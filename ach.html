Achivements:

Common Achivements

The easiest achivements to unlock. Most of these can probably be finished in the first 10 minutes of the game. These encourage you to try for the harder ones.

Locked! (Survive your first month!)

Locked! (Die!)

Locked! (Preach!)

Locked! (Get 150 popularity!)

Locked! (Buy your first jewelery!)

Locked! (Buy a poster pack!)

Rare Achivements

Rarer but not yet hard achievements to get, there is a good chance you will get some of these if you get far on your first playthrough. These can be finished within an hour if you know what you are doing.

Locked! (Get 2000 popularity!)

Locked! (Spend at least 500 sous in 1 month!)

Locked! (Buy at least 25 bread in one month!)

Locked! (Buy the Herb of Power!)

Locked! (Have an income past 1000 sous!)

Locked! (Beat the game on I'm Too Scared! or higher!)

Locked! (Beat the game on Call me Max! or higher!)

Epic Achievements

Difficult achievements to nab, these are not things you will be awarded in your first playthough. A couple of hours of playing and some rage will get you through these.

Locked! (Beat the game before year 1800 on I'm Too Scared! or higher!)

Locked! (Have all stats drop below 25 in the same month on Call me Max! or higher!)

Locked! (Have more than 2500 as your current balance!)

Locked! (Have an income past 1500!)

Locked! (Beat the game on Vive la Révolution!)

LEGENDARY Achievements

The most painful milestones imaginable. They are difficult beyond belief and will take you days. Only masochists and true dedicated players make it here.

Locked! (Beat the game before 1797 on I'm Too Scared! or higher!)

Locked! (Beat the game on L'État, c'est moi!)
